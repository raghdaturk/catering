Catering PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy oversees the manner in which Ghaseel collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the ghaseel.com website or mobile application ("Site"). This privacy policy covers the use and running of the Site and all services offered by Ghaseel.

Personal identification information

We may acquire personal identification information from Users in a different ways, including when Users visit our site, register on the site, place an order, subscribe to the newsletter, respond to a survey, fill out a form, and in line with other activities, services, features or resources provided by us on our Site. Users may be asked to provide some basic information such as name, email address, mailing address, phone number, credit card information. We will acquire personal identification information from Users only if they voluntarily present such information to us. Users can always refuse to present personally identification information, except that it may hinder them from engaging in some Site associated activities.

Non-personal identification information

We may acquire non-personal identification information about Users anytime they visit or interact with our Site. Non-personal identification information may involve the browser name, the kind of computer and technical information about a User's method of connection to the Site, such as the operating system and the Internet service providers used and other related information.

Web browser cookies

Our Site may use "cookies" in order to ensure the improvement of User experience. User's web browser puts cookies on their hard drive for the sake of record-keeping and sometimes to track information about them. User may decide to set their web browser to decline cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If they do so, note that some parts of the Site may not function properly.

How we use collected information

  • Ghaseel may collect and use Users personal information for the following purposes:
  • To improve customer service - Information you provide helps us respond to your customer service requests and support needs more efficiently.
  • To personalize user experience - We may use information in the aggregate to analyse how our Users generally use the services and resources available on our Site.
  • To enhance our Site - We may use feedback you present to us to enhance services.
  • To process payments - We may use the information Users present to us about themselves when making an order only to provide service to that order. We don't share this information with outside parties unless to the degree necessary to provide the service.
  • To run a promotion, contest, survey or other Site feature - To send Users information they consented to get about topics or information we believe will be of interest as well as relevant to them.
  • To send periodic emails - We may use the email address to send User information and updates regarding their order. It may also be used to provide response or feedbacks to their queries, and/or other requests. If User chooses to opt-in to our mailing list, they will get emails that may involve company news, updates, associated product or service information, etc. If at any time the User would want to unsubscribe from getting future emails, we incorporate unsubscribe instructions at the bottom of each email or User may simply contact us through our Site.

How we protect your information

We use proper data collection, storage and processing methods and security measures to safeguard against unauthorized access, modification, disclosure or damage of your personal information, username, password, transaction information and data stored on the Site.

Any sensitive and private data exchange between the Site and its Users occur over a SSL secured communication channel. Additionally, it is encrypted and safeguarded with digital signatures.

Sharing your personal information

We don't sell, trade, or rent Users personal identification information to anyone. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not associated to any personal identification information as regards visitors and users with our business partners, trusted affiliates and advertisers for the purposes stated above. We may use third-party service providers to assist us in operating our business and the Site or implement activities on our behalf, including sending out newsletters or surveys. We may share your information with these third parties for those limited purposes as long as you have granted us the permission to do so.

By making an order via this site OR over the phone we are authorized to share the personal information presented to us with as many contracted service providers as needed to perform the requested service(s).

Advertising

Ads showing on our site may be delivered to Users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies permit the ad server to identify your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile non-personal identification information about you or others who use your computer. This privacy policy does not cover or govern the use of cookies by any advertisers.

You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html
